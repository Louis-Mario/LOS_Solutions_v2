LOU-SERV — Digital Management Platform

LOS Solutions Ltd developed LOU-SERV: a complete digital management platform built specifically for diagnostic imaging and laboratory centres. It replaces paper registers, manual filing, and scattered WhatsApp messages with one organised system every team member can use from their role. From the moment a patient walks in to the moment their report is delivered, LOU-SERV tracks everything.
